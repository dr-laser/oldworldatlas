Community Contributions to the Atlas

Welcome to the developer mode! This is a special version of the Old World Atlas that allows members of the community to contribute by making suggestions and additions directly on the map.

How it works:

  • Right-click on the map to add a new location or point of interest
  • You can save multiple entries locally before submitting them
  • Your username will be associated with your suggestions

Please enter a username to get started:

Important: Your contributions are stored locally on your device. The data will persist across page refreshes, but will be lost if you clear your browser cache. No data is private - anyone with your username can view or edit your contributions. Please save multiple locations before sending to reduce spam. Thank you for helping improve the atlas!
