Feedback & Corrections

See something wrong or missing? Some functinality you'd like to see? I'd love to hear any feedback, corrections, or suggestions in order to improve this project. The Old World Atlas is only possible with contributions from the community. Feel free to reach out in any of the following ways:
  • Email: atlasoftheoldworld@gmail.com
  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/toddkozlowski/oldworldatlas-web/issues
  • Discord: @toddkoz
Or use the form below to submit feedback directly and anonymously:
Form submissions are processed via Web3Forms, a third-party form handling service. No analytics, tracking, or advertising are involved.

What to Report

We appreciate reports on:

  • Incorrect settlement locations, names, populations
  • Missing settlements or points of interest
  • Technical issues or bugs with the map interface
  • Suggestions for new features or improvements
Although not required, providing a specific lore source when reporting inaccuracies is helpful and will improve the project's accuracy and consistency.

Thank you for your support!
